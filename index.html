Welcome

Lazy Ranch has been raising top quality ranch and performance horses for six generations in northern Utah. Titus Davis immigrated to northern Utah in 1863 and started ranching with the finest horses of his day. His son, Thomas Ap Davis, ranched on the Promontory range and followed his father’s tradition by adding to the Davis Ranch remuda any improved stock he could acquire in those early days.
